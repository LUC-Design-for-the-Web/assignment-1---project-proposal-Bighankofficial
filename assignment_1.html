Inspiration for this Site

At one point in time, ceiling lights and desk lamps were the pinnacle of office lighting. But then, the designers at Razer decided to take things to the next level as they introduced RGB into all of their tech designs. On their site, you will find a dark atmosphere where they are able to highlight the amazing features of RGB lighting. This is where I found inspiration to bring you Neon Design Inc.

Page 1 Home

On page 1 or the home screen, it will display various pictures that are linked to RGB lights for that particular topic. This will give the customers an idea of what types of RGB lights we work with so they can begin to plan their setting.

Page 2 Designer Page

On page 2 you can select the RGB products you want utilized and where you want them installed. This will give the customer multiple options from RGB lights for PC components to RGB wall decoration.
