Inspiration for this Site

At one point in time, desk lamps were the pinnacle of office lighting. But then, the designers at Razer decided to take things to the next level as they introduced RGB into all of their tech designs. On their site, you will find a dark atmosphere where they are able to highlight the amazing features of RGB lighting. This is where I found inspiration to bring you Neon Design Inc.
